Hello Everyone!


My name is Akshita Maradapu Vera Venkata Sai. Welcome to my webpage. I'm a 3rd year Ph.D. student working with Dr. Yingshu Li and Dr. Zhipeng Cai in the Department of Computer Science at Georgia State University. I have completed my Masters from Georgia State University in 2017 with research focused on VNE and Security and Privacy under Dr.Xiaojun Cao and B.Tech from GITAM University, India in 2016. I've been the Membership Chair of IEEE Student Chapter in GSU since 2018 where I work towards designing strategies to get more students involved in CS research and IEEE Society.
My area of research includes Security and Privacy, Social networks, Mobile Crowd Sensing Systems and Deep Learning. My current research is on privacy issues in Mobile Social networks and MCS and solving them using ML techniques.
